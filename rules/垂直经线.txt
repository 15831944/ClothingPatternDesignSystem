规则 垂直经线
输入 点 upPoint
输入 参数 length

点 right = (1,0)
点 down = (0,1)
点 doPoint = 求偏移(upPoint, length, down)
点 upV = 求偏移(upPoint, 0.5, right)
upV = 求偏移(upV, 0.5, down)
点 doV = 求偏移(doPoint, -0.5, right)
doV = 求偏移(doV, -0.5, down)

路径 wline = upV 连接 upPoint 连接 doPoint 连接 doV
wline = wline.标记(经向)

wline = wline.命名(经线)

输出 wline
